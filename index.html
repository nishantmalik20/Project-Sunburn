Event

Sunburn festival is premier Electronic Music Festival hosting Asia’s largest 3-day Electronic Music Festival. We began our journey in 2007 and aimed to make it bigger and better and strive to do so till date. We are honoured to have artists like Martin Garrix, Dimitri Vegas, Like Mike, Afrojack, Tiesto, DJ Snake and many more. An exhilarating memorable experience in the city of Pune. The headliner announcement included the top artists lIke Axwell Ingrosso, Armin van Buuren, DJ Snake and Alan Walker. In 2017, Sunburn festival’s second decade started with a bang! The headliner announcement included Martin Garrix, Afrojack, DJ Snake, Dimitri Vegas and Like Mike, Clean Bandit, KSHMR, Kayzo, Infected Mushroom and 90 plus supporting acts. For 2016’s biggest celebration, Sunburn Festival comes to the city of Pune, with a Phase 1 headliner announcement including Afrojack, Armin van Buuren, Axwell & Ingrosso, Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike, and KSHMR, plus nearly 50 acts supporting.
